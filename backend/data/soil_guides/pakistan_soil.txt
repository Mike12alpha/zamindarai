Pakistan Soil Fertilizer Guide

WHEAT (Gandum)
Soil Type: Loamy to clay loam
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen (N): 90-110 kg (2 bags Sona Urea)
- Phosphorus (P): 60-80 kg (1.5 bags Engro DAP)
- Potassium (K): 40-50 kg (1 bag SOP)
Application Timing:
1. At sowing: Full DAP + half SOP as basal
2. First irrigation (21 days): Half urea
3. Second irrigation (45 days): Remaining urea + half SOP
Micronutrients: Zinc sulfate 10 kg/acre if deficiency observed

RICE (Dhan / Chawal)
Soil Type: Heavy clay with good water retention
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen: 100-120 kg
- Phosphorus: 50-60 kg
- Potassium: 50-60 kg
Application Timing:
1. Basal: Full P + half K
2. Tillering: Half N
3. Panicle initiation: Half N + half K
Special: Apply silica for basmati varieties

COTTON (Kapas)
Soil Type: Sandy loam to medium loam
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen: 120-150 kg
- Phosphorus: 60-70 kg
- Potassium: 50-60 kg
Application Timing:
1. Basal: Full P + half K
2. Squaring: One-third N
3. Flowering: One-third N
4. Boll formation: One-third N + half K
Note: Boron deficiency common — apply borax 5 kg/acre

SUGARCANE (Ganna)
Soil Type: Deep fertile loam
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen: 150-175 kg
- Phosphorus: 75-100 kg
- Potassium: 100-125 kg
Application Timing:
1. Basal: Full P + half K
2. 30 days: One-third N
3. 60 days: One-third N
4. 90 days: One-third N + half K

MAIZE (Makai)
Soil Type: Well-drained loam
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen: 100-120 kg
- Phosphorus: 60-70 kg
- Potassium: 50-60 kg
Application Timing:
1. Basal: Full P + half K + one-third N
2. Knee-high stage: One-third N
3. Tasseling: One-third N + half K

TOMATO (Tamatar)
Soil Type: Sandy loam with pH 6.0-6.8
NPK Requirements (per acre):
- Nitrogen: 80-100 kg
- Phosphorus: 80-100 kg
- Potassium: 100-120 kg
Application Timing:
1. Basal: Full P + half K
2. Flowering: Half N + half K
3. Fruit set: Half N
